FAQ

Where is my data stored? Entirely in your own Apple account's private iCloud via Apple CloudKit — no external server. See the Privacy Policy.

How do I share with staff? The clinic owner shares an iCloud link; staff open it to join, then the owner approves them in the app.

Does it need internet? It works offline; data syncs to iCloud when online.

How do I cancel my subscription? Manage it in your device's App Store settings.
